PCB Layout

Marconi use cutting edge technologies and industry leading software for PCB layouts.

We will provide all the necessary files to enable the PCB to be manufactured from the customer's preferred supplier, or we can source and supply PCBs as required - including prototypes and full production quantities.

PCBs can be designed to meet the budget and size contraints for the customers's product.

All Manufacturability and testability requirements will be incorporated into the final design. Test rigs can also be developed in order to automatically test the PCB for high volume manufacture.
