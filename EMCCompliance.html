EMC Compliance

With the proliferation of high-speed digital circuitry, high-current switchmode power supplies and vairous forms of wireless connectivity, electromagnetic problems are now abundant for poorly designed PCBs.

Marconi design each PCB to achieve the best EMC performance. Including:

  • PCB layouts for EMC compliance by design
  • Prevention of Electromagnetic Interference (EMI Filtering)
  • Prevention of conducted emmissions - adopting power supply filtering
  • Solutions for Electromagnetic susceptability (EMS)

Marconi can provide the requisite testing for compliance - ensuring the the correct certification is in place for each region where the product is to be sold.

Marconi can also provide a consultancy service to track down and fix existing EMC problems that the customer may have in their products.
