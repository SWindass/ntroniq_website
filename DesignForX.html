Design for X

Marconi Electronics prides itself on creating excellent designs that enable the customer to achieve their ambitions.

We incorporate design excellence at every stage of our process to ensure the the final product not only works but is manufacturable, testable and reliable.

Design for Manufacture

PCBs are designed to provide ease of manufacture, ensuring that the manufacturing process has few defects and is therefore high quality and low cost.

Design for Test

PCBs can be designed to incorporate automated testing, including:

  • Self-Test
  • In-circuit testing (ICT)
  • Automated Optical Inspection (AOI)

Design for Reliablity

Our solutions are designed to meet the reliability requirements of the customer.

We perform predicted failure analysis including:

  • MTBF calculations (Mean Time Between Failure)
  • FMEA Analysis (Failure Mode and Effects)

Design for Quality

The product quality has a direct impact on the products success.

Poor quality products cost more to manufacture and support, and they provide the end user with a poor experience.

Marconi prides itself in creating high quality products by incorporation quality checks at all stages of the design, and by building quality checks into the production system from the beginning.

Design for Sustainability

The focus of DFS is to reduce the carbon footprint as much as possible, through careful component selection utilising recycled materials and green manufacturing processes.

Design for Cost

The overall cost of the product is considered from the start. This includes the development cost and the ongoing cost of production. Individual component costs and component availability are considered at this stage - ensuring that the final product achieves the functional requirements and that manufacturing delays caused by supply problems are minimised.

Other key considerations include the time-to-market, component obsolescence (leading to redesign).
