Design Expertise

Marconi Electronics will work with you to define the shape of your project. Including gathering requierments and defining a delivery plan.

We will help you to define what needs to be done, to ensure successful delivery of your product, we can guide you through the design process including:

  • Formal Specification
  • Schematic design
  • Simulations
  • MTBF predictions
  • FMEA analysis
  • PCB Layout
  • Testing
  • Prototype / Full production quantities

We are expert in complex analog design and high-speed digital design, and can help you with projects involving:

  • Microprocessor circuitry
  • High-speed memory interfaces
  • FPGA integration
  • ASIC integration
  • Microcontrollers
  • DACs
  • Ethernet, I2C, RS232/485
  • Switch Mode Power supplies
  • EMI filter design
  • RF Filter design (high-pass, low-pass, band-pass, band-stop)
  • Surge protection
  • ADCs
  • Motor Control
  • EMC Compliance
